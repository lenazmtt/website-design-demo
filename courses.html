The Malta College of Arts, Science and Technology (MCAST) offers a wide and practical range of courses designed to prepare students for real-world careers and further study. Its programme portfolio includes over 200 full-time courses at levels from introductory certificates and diplomas to Bachelor’s, Master’s, and even Doctoral degrees, spanning key fields such as engineering, applied science, information technology, business, health and social care, creative arts, and trades. These courses are delivered through hands-on, work-based learning across several institutes and also at the Gozo campus, allowing students to build both theoretical knowledge and practical skills relevant to industry needs.
